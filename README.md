# Apex-Retail-Sales-Dashboard
## Dashboard Preview

[Apex Retail Sales Dashboard Preview](<Apex Dashboard.png>)


An interactive retail sales dashboard built in Microsoft Excel to analyze sales performance, profitability, product categories, regional trends, and payment methods. The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into actionable business insights through interactive filtering and visual reporting.

## Project Overview

The objective of this project was to transform retail sales data into a clear and user-friendly dashboard that supports business decision-making. The dashboard provides an overview of key performance indicators (KPIs), sales trends, category performance, payment methods, and salesperson performance.

Users can interact with the dashboard through slicers to explore different business segments and identify trends across regions, categories, and payment modes.

## Dashboard Features

### Interactive Filters
The dashboard includes slicers that allow users to dynamically filter data by:

- Region (East, North, South, West)
- Payment Mode (Card, Cash, Online)
- Product Category

### Sales by Product Category
Visualizes total sales across product categories, helping identify the strongest revenue-generating segments.

### Profit by Category
Displays the contribution of each product category to overall profit.

### Payment Mode Analysis
Analyzes customer payment preferences and transaction volume by payment type.

### Top Performing Salespersons
Highlights the top sales representatives and their contribution to overall sales performance.

### Daily Sales Trend
Tracks sales performance over time and helps identify seasonal patterns and revenue fluctuations.

---

## Key Business Insights

- Electronics generated the highest sales revenue among all product categories.
- Online payments represented a significant portion of customer transactions.
- A small group of top-performing sales representatives contributed a large share of overall sales.
- Daily sales trends revealed fluctuations that can help support future forecasting and planning.
